Generate a pairing code to link your WhatsApp bot instantly.

How to Link Your Bot:

  1. Enter your **WhatsApp number** (full international format, no `+` or spaces).
  2. Click **"Generate Pairing Code"**.
  3. Open **WhatsApp** on your primary phone.
  4. Go to **Linked Devices** > **Link a Device**.
  5. Tap on **"Link with phone number instead"**.
  6. Enter the **pairing code** displayed above.
  7. Once connected, the bot will send your session string to your WhatsApp. Keep it safe!
